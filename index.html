Welcome

Welcome to the home page for Mark Huberty. I'm a graduate student in the Travers Department of Political Science at UC Berkeley, and a Research Associate at the Berkeley Roundtable on the International Economy.

My political science research spans three somewhat distinct areas: climate change and the politics of energy systems transformations; technological change and political contestation; and statistical and computational methods. You can find more on my research, as well as working papers, at my research page.
